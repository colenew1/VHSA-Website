What's Required by Texas Law

Understanding the screening requirements for each grade level helps ensure your school stays compliant.

Important Notes:

  • • New students at any grade level must complete all required screenings for their grade and gender.
  • • Scoliosis screening is required at different ages for girls vs. boys in order to screen them during rapid growth phases. Girls are screened in 5th and 7th grades because they mature earlier and faster. Boys are screened in 8th grade as scoliosis can progress more slowly.
  • • Students should be screened with glasses on, so it is important that they bring them on the day of testing.
