Meet Erin Newman, PPCNP-BC

Hi, I'm Erin Newman, a Pediatric Primary Care Nurse Practitioner (PPCNP-BC). I started Vision & Hearing Screening of Austin in 2008 because I believe every child deserves access to quality health screenings. Over the past 15+ years, our team has screened more than 20,000 students across the greater Austin area.

As a board-certified pediatric nurse practitioner, I bring specialized expertise in child health and development to every screening. What started as a passion for helping kids has grown into trusted partnerships with over 150 schools. We love what we do, and it shows in how we work with students‚Äîmaking each screening a positive, comfortable experience.

My clinical background in pediatric primary care gives me unique insight into the importance of early detection and intervention. That's why we've built our business on the foundation of care, professionalism, and genuine concern for each child's wellbeing.
