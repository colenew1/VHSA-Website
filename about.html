Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from school administrators about our health screening services.

Vision & Hearing Screening of Austin (VHSA) was started in 2008 when an Austin private school reached out and requested help from Erin Newman, owner and founder. She went through classes, became DSHS certified, and grew the business to serve over 100 schools across the greater Austin area.

Yes, our screeners are all certified by the Texas Department of State Health Services for vision screening, hearing screening, and scoliosis screening. They are certified through the Texas Border Health Office for acanthosis nigricans screening. They all have additional training in ethics and integrity through the Catholic Diocese of Austin.

We provide all of the health screenings required by Texas state law including vision, hearing, acanthosis nigricans, and spinal screening.

DSHS requires each school to report Vision, Hearing and Spinal Screening results between January 15 and June 30. For online data entry, go to chrstx.dshs.texas.gov. Acanthosis Nigricans results must be reported to the Texas Risk Assessment for Type 2 Diabetes in Children at rfes.utpa.edu by March 31st.

Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your school's information.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide health screening services to your school, including:

  • School contact information and administrative details
  • Student screening results and health data as required by Texas state law
  • Communication records for scheduling and follow-up purposes

How We Use Your Information

All information is used solely for:

  • Conducting required health screenings
  • Generating reports for school records and state compliance
  • Scheduling and coordinating screening services
  • Communicating screening results to appropriate school personnel

Information Protection

We maintain strict confidentiality standards:

  • All screening data is handled in compliance with HIPAA and FERPA regulations
  • Information is shared only with authorized school personnel
  • Physical and digital records are secured and access is limited
  • We never sell or share student information with third parties

Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy practices or need to request information about your school's data, please contact us at erin@screenaustin.com.
