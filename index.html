Vision Screening

State-mandated vision acuity testing for all grade levels. We use equipment recommended by the Department of State Health Services to ensure accurate results and early detection. We provide accurate testing on children as young as three years old.

Hearing Screening

Pure tone audiometry screening for early detection of hearing issues. Our audiometers are registered by DSHS and calibrated annually. Our kid-friendly approach makes the process comfortable and stress-free.

Scoliosis Screening

Spinal curvature screening for early intervention. Required for 5th and 7th grade girls, and 8th grade boys. We conduct these screenings with sensitivity and privacy, ensuring students feel comfortable throughout the process.

Acanthosis Nigricans Screening

Skin screening for early diabetes risk detection. This important screening helps identify potential health concerns early, allowing for timely intervention.
