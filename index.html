Our Services

Comprehensive health screening services that meet Texas state requirements and keep your students healthy.

Vision Screening Icon

Vision Screening

Required for All Grades

State-mandated vision acuity testing for all grade levels. We use professional equipment to ensure accurate results and early detection of vision issues.

Hearing Screening Icon

Hearing Screening

Required for All Grades

Pure tone audiometry screening for early detection of hearing issues. Our kid-friendly approach makes the process comfortable and stress-free.

Scoliosis Screening Icon

Scoliosis Screening

Required for 5th, 7th, 8th Grade

Spinal curvature screening for early intervention. We conduct these screenings with sensitivity and privacy, ensuring students feel comfortable throughout the process.

Acanthosis Screening Icon

Acanthosis Screening

Required for 1st, 3rd, 5th+

Skin screening for early diabetes risk detection. This important screening helps identify potential health concerns early, allowing for timely intervention.
